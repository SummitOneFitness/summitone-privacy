SummitOne Fitness

SummitOne is an all-in-one, user-led personal training platform designed to help users take control of their fitness journey. The app combines workout programming, nutrition tracking, and progress monitoring into a single streamlined experience.

Built for gym-goers, busy professionals, and anyone serious about improving their health, SummitOne focuses on flexibility and user choice rather than rigid coaching systems.

## Features

- **User-Led Personal Training**
  - Customize your own fitness journey based on goals, schedule, and preferences.

- **Workout Program Generator**
  - Automatically create structured workout programs tailored to your available time and training style.

- **Custom Workout Builder**
  - Build and log individual workouts and exercises.

- **Nutrition Tracking**
  - Log food and nutrition data to support fitness and health goals.

- **Barcode Scanning**
  - Scan food barcodes using your device camera to quickly retrieve nutrition information.

- **Progress Tracking**
  - Monitor workout history, performance trends, and improvements over time.

- **Progress Photos**
  - Track visual progress and stay motivated.

- **Daily Checklists**
  - Stay consistent with daily fitness and nutrition goals.

## Target Users

SummitOne is designed for:

- Gym-goers
- Busy adults balancing work and fitness
- Individuals who want flexible, customizable workout planning
- Anyone focused on improving their health and performance
