SummitOne Fitness ("SummitOne", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and protects information when you use the SummitOne app.

Information We Collect

When using SummitOne, users may provide information related to their fitness journey, including workout data, nutrition logs, progress photos, workout history, and custom programs.

Camera Access

The SummitOne app may request access to your device camera. Camera access is used solely for scanning food product barcodes so the app can retrieve nutritional information. The camera is only used when a user actively chooses to scan a barcode.

Device Information

Basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app performance data may be collected to maintain and improve the functionality of the application.

How We Use Information

  • Provide workout tracking and training program features
  • Enable nutrition logging and barcode scanning
  • Track progress and performance trends
  • Improve app reliability and user experience

Data Sharing

SummitOne does not sell, rent, or trade personal user data. Barcode numbers may be sent to third-party nutrition databases only for retrieving food information.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information. However, no digital platform can guarantee complete security.

Children's Privacy

SummitOne is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.
